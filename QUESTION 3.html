Brief history of Lilian Ngoyi

Masebida Ngoyi was born in Pretoria in 1911to a Bapedi family of six children. She initially did teaching training at the Kilnerton Training Institution. When her father could no longer afford the fees, she began training as a nurse at City Deep Mine hospital. Ultimately, she took employment in a government in a garment factory between 1945 and 1956 , where she joined and became active in the Garment Workers Union, under the leadership of Solly Sachs.
